Educational Scholarship Advisor

Helping students in Rwanda and Africa find scholarships easily

Use Case Description

The Educational Scholarship Advisor helps students in Rwanda and Africa check if they qualify for scholarships. Students answer simple questions, and the system shows which scholarships they can apply for.

Stakeholders

  • Students: Main users seeking scholarships.
  • Schools and Universities: Provide scholarship opportunities.
  • Government and NGOs: Fund scholarships.
  • Companies: Offer scholarships to support education.
  • Advisors/Counselors: Help students apply for scholarships.
